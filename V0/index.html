Brief Bio

I was born in June 1987 in Athens, Greece. I finished school in Chania, Crete and successfully admitted in the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Technical University of Crete (TUC). I obtained the Diploma (5-year Bachelor's program) degree and the MSc. Degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering in Octomber 2011 and in July 2014 respectively, under the guidance of Assistant Professor Aggelos Bletsas.

Currently, I am a a research assistant at the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Technical University of Crete, Greece. I am involved in several research projects and real-world application challenges. My current funding comes from the BLASE project. Finally, in Autumn I will join the group of Prof. Matt Reynolds to begin my PHD studies at the University of Washington.
